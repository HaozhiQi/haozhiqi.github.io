I am a Research Scientist at Amazon Frontier AI & Robotics (FAR) and an incoming Assistant Professor in the Computer Science Department at UChicago. My research lies at the intersection of robot learning, computer vision, and tactile sensing. My Ph.D. thesis focused on Multisensory Dexterity for Robotics.
I received my Ph.D. from UC Berkeley, where I worked with Prof. Yi Ma and Prof. Jitendra Malik. I received my Bachelor's degree from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), where I was advised by Prof. Chi-Keung Tang. I also spent two years at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA), working on computer vision with Dr. Jifeng Dai and Dr. Yichen Wei.
